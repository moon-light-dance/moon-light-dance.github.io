What can I do for you?

Greetings!

This page is primarily for those who are intrested in hiring my services. I have around 3 years of experience in digital pixel art & 2-D animation, and I'm constantly learning new things and improving my skills. As you're reading this, I'm animating!

If you want to see what I've made, check out my Portfolio page for my best work.

I have experience in storyboarding, pose-to-pose and straight-ahead animation, as well as coloring, shading, and clean-up. I'm happy with making entire animations, or being a part of a larger project!

Workflow

The process usually looks something like this: First I make a basic layout of how things move. Camera angles, major character poses, annotations, this is called a storyboard animatic. Next, I add breakdown frames to flesh out the movements and dynamics of the animation. Once everything is looking smooth, I start cleaning up the animation. Redraw, refine, and repeat until I'm satisfied. After I'm done with clean up, I start coloring and shading! Usually I do these with the characters first, and then I move on to backgrounds later.
